**Dataset**<br>
Data: Global_Emissions_CH4_CO_MCF_2020.nc<br>
Contact: Xueying Yu (xyu2@albany.edu); Dylan Millet (dbm@umn.edu)<br>

**Description**<br>
Global emissions of methane, carbon monoxide, and methyl chloroform for the year 2020. Emission estimates are derived using a four-dimensional variational (4D‑Var) data assimilation in the GEOS-Chem Adjoint Model that integrates space‑based observations of methane, formaldehyde (HCHO), and carbon monoxide from the TROPOspheric Monitoring Instrument (TROPOMI), along with in situ NOAA measurements of methyl chloroform.<br>
Spatial resolution: 4° × 5°<br>
Temporal resolution: monthly<br>
Time period: Jan 2020 – Dec 2020<br>

Variables<br>
EMIS_CH4: Global methane emissions from TROPOMI-based methane oxidation chain assimilation<br>
EMIS_CH4_UNC: Uncertainties of the global methane emissions<br>
EMIS_CO: Global CO emissions from TROPOMI-based methane oxidation chain assimilation<br>
EMIS_CO_UNC: Uncertainties of the global CO emissions<br>
EMIS_MCF: Global MCF emissions from TROPOMI-based methane oxidation chain assimilation<br>
EMIS_MCF_UNC: Uncertainties of the global MCF emissions<br>
